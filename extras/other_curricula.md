# Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Epistemology independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

- [Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy: Epistemology Entry](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/epistemology/) (comprehensive overview and bibliography)
- [MIT OpenCourseWare Philosophy Courses](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/linguistics-and-philosophy/)
- [Open Yale Courses: Philosophy](https://oyc.yale.edu/philosophy)
- [Class Central: Epistemology Courses](https://www.classcentral.com/subject/epistemology) (curated list of free online courses)
